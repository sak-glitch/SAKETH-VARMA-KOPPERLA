Why SoftFlow?

We saw our friends losing hair in Bangalore. So we built a solution.

👋 The Bangalore Hard Water Story

Bangalore's water is notoriously hard. With TDS levels often exceeding 300 ppm (and sometimes hitting 500+), the city's groundwater is packed with calcium and magnesium salts. For a city that attracts students from across the country, this creates a silent crisis.

We noticed it first in the bathroom drain. Clumps of hair. Dry, itchy skin. White scale on taps. Our friends in PGs across Koramangala, Indiranagar, and HSR Layout were all complaining about the same thing — their hair was falling out faster than ever.

Dermatologists confirmed: hard water is a major contributor to hairfall and skin issues. The minerals in hard water react with shampoos and soaps, forming a sticky residue that clogs pores and weakens hair follicles. The solution? Soft water.

💡 The SoftFlow Idea

But here's the problem — most water softeners are expensive, require permanent installation, and need a plumber. As PG students, we couldn't modify our landlord's plumbing. We needed something portable, affordable, and tool-free.

So we built SoftFlow. A filter that screws onto any tap in seconds, gives you soft water instantly, and fits in your bag when you move out. No plumber. No permanent changes. No drama.

🎯 Our Mission

We're on a mission to make soft water accessible to every student and young professional in Bangalore. 10,000+ filters sold and counting — and every review of softer hair and clearer skin keeps us going.
