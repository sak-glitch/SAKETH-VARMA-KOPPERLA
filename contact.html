Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

Does it fit my PG tap?

Yes! SoftFlow works with all standard taps found in Bangalore PGs and hostels. No modifications needed.

How long does the cartridge last?

Each cartridge lasts ~30 days or 1,000 litres. We recommend monthly replacement for best results.

Can I take it when I move out?

Absolutely! Just unscrew it and put it in your bag. That's the whole point — it's portable.

Do I need a plumber to install?

No! Just hand-tighten onto your tap. No tools, no plumber, no permanent changes to your room.
