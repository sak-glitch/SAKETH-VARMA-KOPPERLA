How SoftFlow Works

Three simple steps to get soft, filtered water in your PG room — no plumber, no tools, no drama.

1

Screw Onto Any Tap

SoftFlow features a universal threading system that fits all standard taps found in Bangalore PGs, hostels, and apartments. Simply line it up and hand-tighten — no wrenches, no tools, no plumber required. It takes 10 seconds.

  • ✓ Fits bathroom and kitchen taps
  • ✓ No modifications to existing plumbing
  • ✓ Works with both round and flat spouts
2

Get Filtered Soft Water

Once attached, turn on the tap as usual. Water passes through SoftFlow's multi-stage filtration system that removes:

↓ 90% Calcium & Magnesium
